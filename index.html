What is Avalanche Cross Chain?

Core Bridge is the app-first route for moving supported assets between external networks and the Avalanche ecosystem. Avalanche is a multi-chain platform with a Primary Network of X-Chain, C-Chain, and P-Chain, described in the Avalanche Primary Network docs.

How Avalanche Cross Chain works

  1. Open CoreGo to the verified Core Bridge URL and connect a compatible wallet.
  2. Choose routeSelect the source network, destination network, and supported asset shown in the app.
  3. Review detailsCheck the domain, token, receiving address, route, and any wallet prompts before signing.
  4. Confirm in walletApprove only the transactions you understand, then track the transfer from the app or explorer.

Avalanche Cross Chain FAQ

What is avalanche cross chain?

It refers to moving assets or messages across Avalanche-connected networks, including routes that involve Avalanche C-Chain, Avalanche L1s, or external chains through supported bridge infrastructure.

Where do I open avalanche cross chain app?

Use the official Core Bridge app at core.app/bridge and verify the domain before connecting a wallet.

Does avalanche cross chain use the C-Chain?

Many user-facing Avalanche DeFi routes involve the C-Chain because it is EVM-compatible, but the exact source and destination networks depend on the route shown inside Core.

Is avalanche cross chain a separate wallet?

No. It is an intent around cross-network movement; Core is the wallet and app surface used to access supported bridge routes.

What should I check before an avalanche cross chain transfer?

Verify the URL, selected networks, asset, receiving address, gas asset, and wallet approval text before signing any transaction.
